<p>The collection includes: financial records (Audit Reports approximately 1973-98, ledgers 1944-59, monthly finance reports 1954-67, and Seattle Pacific Foundation documents); building records (Oral V. Hemry files, Miller Science Learning Center records, 'HHFA' Student Union and other miscellaneous property assesments); and selected Blakeley campus records; Casey campus records; capital projects; NCATE accreditation files; NWASC file; Master Plan records; and a subject file.</p>

<scopecontent id="a3" encodinganalog="5202_">
<p>The collection contains 36 letters written by John Newton to John Campbell of Edinburgh, Scotland, and seven copies of letters written by Newton to recipients other than Campbell. All letters date between 1789 and 1802. Newton wrote on theological and personal topics and current events. Some of the letters are stamped for postage and have sealing wax residue on them.</p>
</scopecontent>

<p>This collection contains 46 cassette tapes recording 33 interviews completed by C. Dorr Demaray. Also included are typed transcripts of the interviews.</p>
</scopecontent>
<scopecontent encodinganalog="5202_">
<p>The bulk of this collection is comprised of Cascade College records and other materials, 1939-1969. It also contains North Pacific Evangelical Institute catalogs 1918-1930, Portland Bible Institute catalogs 1930-1939, and 25 class and all-school photographs 1919-1939, was well as Cascade College photographs.</p>
<p>Cascade College records include: accreditation reports; alumni and student records; faculty and student writings; general records; mementos; publications including Cascade College Bulletin 1952-1969, Cascade College Lookout 1941-1969, Count Down, The Portrait, The Chalk Board (Education Office), The Cascade Pathfinder, The Cascade Idea, Cascade Intercessor, Faculty Manual, Student Handbook, yearbooks (various names) 1934-1968; photographs; theses and senior research papers presented to Dr. Lee M. Nash for History Courses 1961-1967, and others; trophies, and historical information.</p>
<p>The collection also contains bound and manuscript copies of Our College: A History of Cascade College, 1918-1957 by Sophia E. Townsend, and a 23-minute video titled Campus Life at Cascade College.</p>
<p>An inventory and subject index to this collection is available.</p>
